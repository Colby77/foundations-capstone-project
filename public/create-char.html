Instructions


Welcome to the character creator! Making a character in D&D is quite simple but a bit of a process for beginners. Be patient and if you need a little help visit the 'Resources' tab above for extra guides.

First, start by picking your character' race and class.

Then there are a couple options you can use for determining your characte's attribute and skill scores. You will pick your character's background/backstory at this step, too.

To wrap it up you will pick your character's alignment (like their moral compass), starting equipment, starting spells (if any) and name.
